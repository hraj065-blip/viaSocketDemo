Risk Register

Risks we've anticipated
and mitigated.

Risk Level Impact Mitigation
Pilot-to-paid conversion rate below 50% Medium Phase 01 revenue target missed Run 50 pilots (not 30) to compensate for lower conversion. Focus ROI reports on the 2 most quantifiable metrics: hours saved and errors eliminated.
Engineering capacity bottleneck High Delivery delays, client dissatisfaction Cap pilot cohorts at 10/month initially. Hire third engineer in Month 2 contingent on Month 1 conversion data. Triage workflows by complexity before committing.
Marketplace low adoption in Phase 02 Medium One-time service revenue delayed Seed marketplace with managed clients first (they already trust us). Launch with guaranteed 48hr SLA on express services — creates differentiation from freelance alternatives.
Partner quality control in Phase 03 High Brand damage if partners deliver poorly Stringent certification exam + practical assessment. Monthly quality scores tracked via client NPS. 3-strikes-and-removal policy. Co-review all partner deliverables in first 30 days.
Competitor response (Zapier, Make) Low Price war on platform tier Services layer is not replicable in months by product companies. Indian market depth (WhatsApp, GST, UPI) creates further moat. First-mover in services creates client lock-in before competitors react.
